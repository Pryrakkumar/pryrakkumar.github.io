Publication

Public Perception of Air Pollution Exposure and Health Impacts in Central Delhi

Kumar, P., & Abhishek, A. (2026). Journal of Emerging Technologies and Innovative Research, 13(2).
DOI: 10.56975/jetir.v13i2.575762

This study examines how residents in Central Delhi perceive air pollution, their awareness of PM2.5-related health impacts, and their preparedness to adopt protective behaviour. The work highlights gaps between measured air quality and public perception, with implications for environmental communication and policy.
