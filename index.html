Publication

Public Perception of Air Pollution Exposure and Health Impacts in Central Delhi

P. Kumar & A. Abhishek, Journal of Emerging Technologies and Innovative Research, 13(2), 2026.

DOI: 10.56975/jetir.v13i2.575762

This study explores how residents in Central Delhi perceive air pollution, their awareness of PM2.5-related health impacts, and their willingness to adopt protective behaviours. The work combines survey data with environmental information to highlight gaps between measured pollution levels and public perception.
